Vocabulary

In this document, "we," "us," and "our" refer to the creator of this project UnnamedBruh.

Information We Collect

We value your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding how we handle your information.

When you upload files to this project, we do not collect, store, or sell any data associated with these files. This includes both the uploaded files and any audio or outputs generated from those files.

Additionally, we do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. Your privacy is our priority, and we are dedicated to ensuring that your data remains secure and untracked.
